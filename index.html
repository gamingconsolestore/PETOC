Petoc: Master Terms and Conditions (Effective Feb 22, 2026)

I. TERMS FOR CUSTOMERS (PET OWNERS)

The Service Model (The Mediator): Petoc is a third-party service provider and digital marketplace, operating on a model similar to global tech aggregators and logistical platforms. We act strictly as a bridge between pet owners and independent service providers.

Not a Veterinary Facility: Petoc is not a veterinary clinic, hospital, or medical practice. We do not employ vets directly to treat animals; we facilitate your connection to external professionals.

Exclusive Communication: To ensure service quality, all coordination must be done through our official WhatsApp channel. Petoc acts as the sole mediator; customers are not permitted to contact listed clinics directly for Petoc-arranged services.

Automatic Agreement: By accessing this website or utilizing any Petoc service or product, you are automatically agreeing to these Terms and Conditions and the Disclaimer in full.

Service Charges: A platform/service fee is applied to every transaction, product purchase, or service booking.

2. Medical, Pharmacy & Health Disclaimer

No Medical Practice: Petoc is never involved in medical procedures, prescriptions, or clinical guidance. Professional responsibility rests solely with the independent vet.

Liability: Petoc is not responsible for mishaps, side effects, or treatment outcomes (clinic, mobile, or chat-based) before, during, or after the process.

No Guarantee of Success: Petoc does not guarantee the success of any treatment or the accuracy of any diagnosis made by independent partners. The choice of the vet is the user's final decision.

No Drugs/Pharmacy: Petoc and its partners do not deal in the sale of injections, medicines, or pharmacy items through this platform. All vaccinations and tests are referred to the service providers.

Medical News: Research or news shared is for informational purposes only and is sourced from authorized professionals.

3. Payments, Refunds & Logistics

Advance Payments: Certain services or premium products require advance payment, which will be communicated at the time of purchase.

Availability Refunds: If a product or service is unavailable or cannot be delivered by the provider, a 100% refund will be issued.

In-Transit Incidents: In the event of a mishap, accident, or theft during the delivery of a product or service, an 80% refund will be issued, provided the customer agrees to these settlement terms.

No-Cancellation (On Arrival): Once a product or a Mobile Vet reaches your doorstep, the order cannot be canceled. Full payment is mandatory, and advance payments become non-refundable at that stage.

Wait Times: Petoc acts as an agent to find the quickest available professional. We do not guarantee immediate response times or availability for emergency cases.

Pick & Drop Service: A specialized transport service is available. Owners may travel with their pets. This is charged separately, including vehicle, driver, and helper fees based on time consumed.

Product Authenticity: We source pet food from authorized distributors. Issues regarding quality must be addressed with the brand manufacturer directly.

Subscriptions: Users can subscribe to Silver, Gold, or Platinum plans. We accept Online Payments only (Cash Payments are conditional).

II. TERMS FOR PARTNERS (CLINICS & VETS)

  • The Degree Standard: A valid professional degree from the concerned national veterinary council is mandatory. Partners must provide a copy for approval.
  • Pharmacy Ban: Partners are strictly prohibited from selling or prescribing drugs/injections through the Petoc platform.
  • Financial Conduct: Listing fees are charged per the selected plan and are non-refundable and non-adjustable.
  • Mediation: All communication must flow through Petoc. Partners cannot bypass the platform to contact customers directly.
  • Voluntary Exit: Partners or volunteers can request to be de-listed from the website at any time via WhatsApp.

III. PRIVACY, CYBER LAW & GOVERNING LAW

Zero-Data Privacy: Petoc does not have a website backend. We do not collect or store personal data, emails, or passwords. Your data remains on your personal device and WhatsApp. Your interaction is private.

Cyber Law: Any fraudulent activity or misuse will be reported to the FIA Cyber Crime Wing under the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA).

Governing Law: Governed by the laws of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.
